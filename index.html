About

KazDesigns.co.ke designs and builds websites in addition to marketing them. Here is how it all started.

  • 2015-2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Straight out of high school, we decided to venture into online E-Commerce. The problem was that most web design companies were either too expensive or built ugly websites.

    That's when we decided to learn how to build websites. Kaz took a web development course, while Elo took a graphic design course and Mel took an online marketing course.

  • October 2016

    An Agency is Born

    After completing our online courses, we decide to open our own website design agency.

  • January 2017

    Expansion

    We expand our business into more services such as logo designing and social media marketing.

  • Today

    As of today we have built lot's of websites for both small and corporate clients. Let's build your website next or refer a client to us and get 10% of the amount they pay.
